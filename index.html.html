05 — Featured Project

Built at Google

✦ Google Build and Blog Marathon, Hyderabad
Kairo

Kairo is an AI-powered SaaS app that converts Gmail conversations into structured task workflows automatically. Built using Google Gemini AI, it extracts tasks from emails, assigns priority levels, and organises them in a Kanban dashboard — saving knowledge workers 2 to 3 hours per week in manual task tracking.

Google Gemini AI SaaS Product Strategy Go-to-Market
AI Email-to-Task Conversion
Google Gemini AI reads your inbox and automatically extracts action items, deadlines, and priorities from email threads.
Smart Priority Detection
Understands urgency from natural language — "ASAP", "by Friday", "end of month" — and assigns priority levels automatically.
Kanban Dashboard
All tasks surfaced in a drag-and-drop board with filters, search, and real-time status updates.
My Role: Strategy and Positioning
Led product positioning, target user definition, and go-to-market narrative for the product during the hackathon.
